v2 doesn't drop any safeguards. It merges related tasks into single, system-driven stages so applications move with fewer manual hand-offs between offices.

Application intake and verification merge into Receipt & Evaluation.
3 + 4 + 5 → 1
Endorsement, assessment and payment fold into Assessment & Payment of Fees.
6 → 1
Final release becomes Issuance of Requested Documents.
